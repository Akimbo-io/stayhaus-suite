§ 02

The Agents — seven specialists, one studio

01
Agent · Intake● live

Onboarding Agent

Captures everything we need from a new brand in one guided flow.
  • Collects Klaviyo direct access (API key)
  • Brand overview, tone, differentiators
  • Customer pains, desired outcomes, USPs
  • Google Drive folder + brand guidelines
  • Triggers all downstream agents on submit
formsecrets vaultwebhook
02
Agent · Space● live

Notion Dashboard

Each client gets their own internal command center.
  • Duplicates a Notion template on signup
  • Auto-fills brand profile + asset library
  • Flow roadmap: planning → live
  • Campaign calendar + performance snapshots
  • Client + agency see progress in real time
notion apitemplate duplication
03
Agent · Plan● spec

Flow Architect

Builds the visual flow blueprint per brand.
  • Library of Miro master flows: welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase, winback, browse abandonment
  • Selects + personalizes by product type
  • Outputs board + structured spec for design & copy
mirotemplates
04
Agent · Design● spec

Visual Agent — Figma or Canva

Produces email visuals on-brand, ready to translate.
  • Pulls brand assets directly from the client's Drive folder
  • Respects brand guidelines: colors, fonts, imagery style
  • Generates per-section: hero, body, CTA, footer
  • Exports in slices so the translation agent can swap pieces cleanly
figmacanvadrive sync
05
Agent · Copy● live

Copywriting Agent

My existing custom GPT, wired into the pipeline.
  • Inputs: brand profile, ICP insights, USPs, flow context, product details
  • Outputs: subject line, preview text, body, CTAs
  • Versions per flow stage and per product type
custom gptbrand voice
06
Agent · Localize● critical

Translate + Compress

The hard problem: ship the same email in N languages.
  • Chops the email into sections
  • Text → translate in place
  • Image-with-text → nano banana regenerates
  • Aggressive compression for inbox weight
nano bananacompress
07
Agent · Ship● spec

Klaviyo Deploy

Drops finished assets straight into the right Klaviyo account.
  • Authenticates with credentials from onboarding
  • Uploads images to Klaviyo asset library
  • Builds the email inside the right flow / campaign
  • Tags by language, flow, version — leaves as draft
klaviyo apidraft mode
§ 03

The Intake Form — the questions every brand answers

Brand & positioning

01Brand overview — 2–3 sentences. Who you are, your tone, what sets you apart.
02Customer insights — pains, desired outcomes, what motivates them to buy.
03Desired outcome — what people want to achieve with your product.
04Why us — what customers most often say makes them choose you.
05Differentiation — what sets your product apart from the market.
06USPs — clear, concrete list.
07Hard-to-copy advantages — real moats.
08One-sentence pitch — why someone should choose you.

Access & assets

AKlaviyo direct access (API key or shared account)
BMaster Google Drive folder — assets, logos, lifestyle, product photography
CBrand guidelines — fonts, colors, voice rules, do/don'ts
DProduct catalog & categories
ELanguages required
FApprover — who signs off on drafts

One email, six languages, no rebuilds.

⌗
